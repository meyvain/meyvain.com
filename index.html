What I do

Plan & Strategise

I believe in a holistic approach to research. Aiming to examine context along with human behaviour, motivations including the struggles, and adoption as well as satisfaction.

Analyze

Making sense of the clutter, uncovering patterns, and finding ways to improve existing solutions. Shedding a light on the user's underlying story.

Implement Research

Choosing the proper research methodology for each phase of the development stage, and conducting qualitative and quantitative research.

Storyteller

Presenting and articulating ideas as a clear story to follow. Being the voice of the user, thus keeping the user at the centre of every design decision.
